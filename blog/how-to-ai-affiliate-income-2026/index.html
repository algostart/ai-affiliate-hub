Step 2: Set Up Your Content Hub (Week 1-2)

You need a home base where your content lives and ranks on Google. Here's the minimum viable setup:

Option A: Static site (what I use)

A simple static site built with HTML/CSS, deployed free on Vercel or GitHub Pages. Zero hosting costs, blazing fast, great for SEO.

What you need: - A domain name (~$12/year) - A GitHub account (free) - Basic HTML/CSS knowledge (or use an AI tool like Framer)

Option B: WordPress

More flexible but costs money. Good if you want a full blog with plugins.

What you need: - Hosting ($5-10/month) - WordPress + Yoast SEO plugin (free) - A clean theme like Jesuspended or Jesuspended

The key pages you need:

  1. Homepage — your brand, value proposition, featured tools
  2. Blog section — where your SEO content lives
  3. Landing pages — one per affiliate product you promote
  4. Bio/links page — for social media profiles

Step 3: Create Your First 4 Blog Posts (Week 2-3)

Content is the engine. Here are the four types of posts that drive affiliate revenue, ranked by conversion rate:

Post Type 1: Product Review (Highest conversion)

Example: "Vidnoz Review 2026: Is This AI Video Tool Worth It?" - 2,000-3,000 words - Pros/cons, pricing breakdown, who it's for - Include your affiliate link 2-4 times naturally

Post Type 2: Comparison Post

Example: "Vidnoz vs HeyGen: Which AI Video Tool Should You Choose?" - Compare 2-3 tools head-to-head - Feature comparison table - Clear winner for different use cases

Post Type 3: How-To Tutorial

Example: "How to Create Marketing Videos with Vidnoz AI in 10 Minutes" - Step-by-step with screenshots - Shows the tool in action - Natural affiliate link placement ("to follow along, sign up here")

Post Type 4: Listicle/Roundup

Example: "Top 7 AI Tools with Recurring Affiliate Programs" - Cast a wider net for traffic - Rank tools with brief reviews - Multiple affiliate links = more commission sources

Pro tip: Write one of each type for your primary tool. That gives you 4 posts targeting 4 different keywords — all pointing to the same affiliate program.

Step 4: Optimize for SEO (Ongoing)

Every post needs to be discoverable on Google. Here's my SEO checklist:

  • Target one primary keyword per post (check search volume with Ubersuggest or Ahrefs free tier)
  • Include the keyword in: title, first paragraph, one H2, meta description, URL slug
  • Write 2,000+ words — longer content consistently outranks short posts for affiliate keywords
  • Add a comparison table — Google loves structured data
  • Include an FAQ section — targets "People Also Ask" snippets
  • Internal link between your posts — your review links to your tutorial, your listicle links to your reviews

Step 5: Distribute on Social Media (Daily)

SEO takes 2-3 months to kick in. While you wait, drive immediate traffic with social media:

LinkedIn (highest conversion for B2B tools)

  • Post 1x daily about AI tools, productivity, business insights
  • Share snippets from your blog posts with a "link in comments" strategy
  • Join AI-focused LinkedIn groups and add genuine value

Twitter/X

  • Tweet 3-5x daily about AI tool tips, trends, and your experiences
  • Create threads summarizing your blog posts
  • Engage with AI influencers to build your network

Reddit

  • Share value-first posts in r/Entrepreneur, r/SaaS, r/ArtificialIntelligence
  • Answer questions and mention tools only when genuinely helpful
  • Never spam your affiliate links — Reddit will ban you instantly

TikTok

  • Create 30-60 second tool demos and "AI hack" videos
  • The algorithm favors new creators — you can go viral with 0 followers
  • Link to your bio page which links to your reviews

Step 6: Build an Email List (Month 2+)

Email is where the real money is. Here's why:

  • You own your email list (unlike social media followers)
  • Email converts 3-5x higher than social media traffic
  • You can promote new products to your entire list with one click

How to build it: 1. Create a lead magnet: "Free Guide: 10 AI Tools That Pay You Monthly" (PDF or email series) 2. Add email capture forms to your blog posts and landing pages 3. Set up a welcome sequence: 5 emails over 2 weeks introducing your top recommended tools 4. Send weekly newsletters with new content, tool updates, and deals

FAQ

How long does it take to make my first dollar? Most people earn their first commission within 2-4 weeks if they're actively creating content and distributing it on social media. SEO commissions take longer (2-3 months) but are more sustainable.

Do I need to buy the tools I promote? You should at least use the free tier extensively. Promoting tools you've never used leads to generic content that doesn't convert. Most AI tools have generous free plans.

Is AI affiliate marketing saturated? The broad keywords are competitive, but long-tail keywords are wide open. "Best AI tools" has millions of results. "Best AI video generator for real estate agents" has almost none. Go niche.

Can I do this without showing my face? Absolutely. Blog content, Twitter threads, LinkedIn posts, and email marketing don't require showing your face. Even TikTok can be screen recordings of you using the tools.

What's the best platform to start with? If you're writing-focused: start with a blog + LinkedIn. If you prefer video: start with TikTok + YouTube Shorts. Both work — pick the format you'll actually stick with.
