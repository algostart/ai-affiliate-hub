The Creator Stack: Why Framer + Beehiiv AI?

Creators need two things: a place to land and a way to stay in touch. Your website is your home base—your portfolio, your offers, your credibility. Your newsletter is your audience engine—your direct line to subscribers and your most valuable asset.

Framer and Beehiiv AI are built for exactly this. Framer is a design-first website builder that lets you create stunning landing pages and full sites without code. Beehiiv AI is a newsletter platform built for growth, with AI-powered tools to help you write, optimize, and monetize your content.

Together they form what we call The Creator Stack: build your website with Framer, grow your audience with Beehiiv. No technical debt, no clunky integrations, no compromise on design.

Framer Review: Build Your Website

Framer is a website builder that prioritizes design and developer experience. Unlike drag-and-drop builders that feel limiting, Framer gives you full control over layout, animations, and responsiveness while keeping the interface intuitive.

What Framer Does Well

  • Design-forward templates: Modern, clean templates that look professional out of the box
  • AI-assisted design: Built-in AI helps you generate layouts, copy, and images
  • CMS and collections: Built-in content management for blogs, portfolios, and product pages
  • Fast performance: Optimized hosting with global CDN and strong Core Web Vitals
  • Custom domains and SSL: Free on all plans

Beehiiv AI Review: Grow Your Audience

Beehiiv AI is a newsletter platform built for creators who want to grow and monetize. It combines a robust email infrastructure with AI tools that help you write, optimize subject lines, and analyze performance.

What Beehiiv AI Does Well

  • Recommendation network: Built-in discovery engine that surfaces your newsletter to subscribers of similar publications
  • Unlimited sends: No per-email limits on paid plans—send as much as you need
  • 0% commission on paid subscriptions: Keep 100% of subscription revenue (minus Stripe fees)
  • Ad network access: Monetize through sponsorships on Scale and Max plans
  • AI-powered writing: AI assists with headlines, body copy, and optimization

How They Work Together

The Creator Stack workflow is simple:

  1. Build your site in Framer — Create a landing page, portfolio, or full site. Add a newsletter signup form or embed that connects to Beehiiv.
  2. Drive traffic to your site — Use SEO, social, or paid ads to bring visitors to your Framer site.
  3. Collect emails with Beehiiv — Embed Beehiiv signup forms or use Beehiiv's landing pages. The recommendation network helps you grow organically.
  4. Nurture and monetize — Send newsletters, launch paid tiers, or monetize through ads—all within Beehiiv.

Integration tip: Beehiiv offers embed forms and API access. You can add a signup form to any Framer page with a simple embed block. Framer's clean design makes your signup CTA stand out without competing with clutter.
